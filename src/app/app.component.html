HIMALAYAN EXPLORER

The Himalayan Explorer is a well-reputed tour and travel agency based in Gangtok. We have been operating for the last 15 years and are renowned for hospitality and customer satisfaction. The driving force behind its establishment was our passion for adventure, to share the beauty of the Himalayas with the outside world, and to promote Sikkim as a premier exotic tourist destination.
